Privacy Policy

How Elite Home Insulation collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

This Privacy Policy describes how Elite Home Insulation ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and handles your personal information when you visit our website or contact us about our services. We are committed to handling your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address when you submit an enquiry form or contact us directly
  • Your property address or suburb when you request a quote
  • Details about your home and the services you're enquiring about
  • Any other information you choose to provide when communicating with us

We only collect information that is reasonably necessary for providing our services and responding to your enquiries.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your quote requests and service enquiries
  • Schedule and coordinate insulation installations
  • Communicate with you about your job or project
  • Send you information about our services where you have requested it
  • Comply with any legal or regulatory obligations

We do not use your personal information for unsolicited marketing purposes, and we do not sell your information to third parties.

Disclosure of Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except where:

  • It is necessary to provide the service you've requested (for example, coordinating with a builder on your behalf)
  • You have given your consent
  • We are required to do so by law

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse, interference, and loss, and from unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Our contact form submissions are handled via standard web protocols. We do not store payment card information.

Access and Correction

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and to request corrections if it is inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date. To make such a request, please contact us using the details below. We will respond to access and correction requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Cookies and Website Analytics

This website does not currently use tracking cookies or third-party analytics services. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
