Our Story

Scotty Williams founded Elite Home Insulation on the Gold Coast with a straightforward mission — making homes more comfortable to live in while reducing energy costs for homeowners.

Specialising in both retrofit and new build insulation, we work across South East Queensland and Northern NSW, supplying and installing wall, ceiling, and acoustic insulation solutions. Whether you're renovating an existing home or building from scratch, Elite Home Insulation delivers quality workmanship and the right product for your space.

We don't believe in overselling or recommending products that aren't right for the job. Every home is different — the roof pitch, wall construction, and the way a space is used all affect which insulation performs best. That's why we assess before we quote, and quote before we start.
