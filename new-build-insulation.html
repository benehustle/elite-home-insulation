Insulation for New Builds Done Right

New residential construction in Queensland must meet the energy efficiency requirements of the National Construction Code (NCC). Insulation is a critical component of achieving the required energy rating, and getting the specification right from the start is far easier — and cheaper — than rectifying it later.

We work directly with builders, project managers, and owner-builders across South East Queensland. We understand construction programmes and the importance of turning up at the right time, completing the work efficiently, and not holding up downstream trades. Whether you have one home or a development of several, we provide a reliable, professional insulation installation service that keeps your build on track.

Common Questions

What insulation R-value is required for new builds in Queensland?

Minimum R-value requirements for new builds in Queensland are set by the NCC and vary by climate zone and construction type. For most of South East Queensland (climate zone 2), a minimum R3.5 is typically required for ceilings. Wall and floor requirements depend on the construction system. Your energy assessor's report will specify the exact requirements for your project, and we install to that specification.

Do you work with volume builders as well as individual homeowners?

Yes. We work with individual homeowners building their first home, small-to-medium builders across South East Queensland, and owner-builders managing their own project. If you're building multiple dwellings or have an ongoing programme of work, get in touch to discuss how we can support your construction schedule.

What documentation do you provide for certifier or council submissions?

On completion we provide written confirmation of the products installed, their R-values, and the areas covered. This documentation is suitable for inclusion in your certifier's compliance file. If your certifier or council requires a specific format or additional information, let us know in advance and we'll accommodate that.
