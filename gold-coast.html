Why Gold Coast Homes Need Good Insulation

Gold Coast summers are long, hot, and humid - ceiling temperatures can exceed 60°C in an uninsulated roof, making air conditioners run almost constantly. Insulating properly is one of the most cost-effective things a Gold Coast homeowner can do. We understand the local climate and the construction styles common to the area, from slab-on-ground brick homes in established suburbs to elevated timber homes closer to the hinterland.
